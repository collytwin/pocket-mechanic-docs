Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 April 2026

Pocket Mechanic is built for UK vehicle owners and independent garages. We take your privacy seriously and explain here what we collect, why, and your rights under UK GDPR.

Who we are

Data controller: Pocket Mechanic Ltd. Email support@pocketmechanic.app for any privacy questions or data requests.

What we collect

  • Vehicle registrations you look up and DVLA / DVSA returned data.
  • Garage records you choose to enter (customer name, phone, address, service log, photos).
  • Diagnostic chats with Spanners AI and the AI replies.
  • Photos you take in the app (vehicles, receipts). Receipt OCR is one-time then discarded.
  • Voice clips: sent to transcription service, discarded once text is returned.
  • Subscription state (tier and renewal). We never see card details.
  • Anonymous crash logs (no personal identifiers).

What we do not collect

No adverts. No analytics trackers. We never sell your data. We do not train AI models on your data.

Third-party processors

DVLA, DVSA, OpenAI, Anthropic, RevenueCat, MongoDB Atlas (UK / EU region). Each receives only the data needed for its feature.

How long we keep it

Garage and service-log records: until you delete them. Chat history: until you delete. MOT cache: 6 hours. Crash logs: 30 days then auto-purged.

Your UK GDPR rights

You can ask us to show, correct, delete, or export your data. Email support@pocketmechanic.app. We respond within 30 days. You can also complain to the Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk.

Children

Not directed at children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes refresh the "Last updated" date above.
