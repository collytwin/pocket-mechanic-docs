Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 April 2026

Pocket Mechanic (the App) is built for UK vehicle owners and independent garages. We take your privacy seriously and keep the data we hold about you to the minimum needed to make the app useful. This page explains exactly what we collect, why, and your rights under the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Who we are

The data controller is Pocket Mechanic Ltd. You can reach us by email at support@pocketmechanic.app for any privacy questions, data subject requests, or to withdraw consent.

Data we collect

  • Vehicle data: registration plates you look up, and the make/model/year/MOT/tax data returned by the DVLA and DVSA APIs. Stored against your account on our secure backend.
  • Garage records: customer names, phone numbers and addresses you choose to enter, plus service-log entries (date, work done, cost, mileage, photos).
  • Diagnostic chats: messages you send to Spanners (our AI assistant), the codes you scan, and the AI's replies. Used to improve the conversation context for that vehicle.
  • Photos: images you take inside the app (vehicle photos, receipts) are encoded and stored in your account. Receipts are sent to the OCR provider for one-time text extraction, then discarded by them.
  • Voice clips: if you use voice input, the audio is sent to the Whisper transcription service and discarded once the text is returned.
  • Subscription state: tier and renewal dates from RevenueCat. We do not see your card details — Apple, Google or RevenueCat handle billing.
  • Crash & usage diagnostics: anonymous error logs from the app and backend. No personal identifiers are tied to these.

What we do not collect

  • We do not display third-party adverts.
  • We do not run analytics trackers like Google Analytics or Facebook Pixel.
  • We do not sell your data to anyone, ever.
  • We do not use your data to train any AI model.

    • DVLA Vehicle Enquiry Service — receives a registration to return basic vehicle data.

      How long we keep it

      • Garage and service-log records: kept until you delete them.

      • Crash logs: 30 days then auto-purged.

      Your rights under UK GDPR

      You can ask us at any time to:

      • Show you the personal data we hold about you (right of access).
      • Correct anything inaccurate (right to rectification).
      • Delete your account and data (right to erasure).
      • Export a copy of your data in JSON (right to portability).
      • Object to or restrict any processing.

      Email us at support@pocketmechanic.app and we will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

      Children

      Pocket Mechanic is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data about anyone under that age.

      Changes to this policy

      We may update this policy from time to time. Any material change will be highlighted on this page with a refreshed "Last updated" date.
